Chapter 10 International Student Information

For incoming international MBAn students, there are 4 key aspects to efficiently prepare you for life at Ross School of Business.

10.1 Student visa and work authorizations

International Center at University of Michigan provides great services regarding these issues. Through phone calls or e-mail, you will get satisfying responses within a couple of days.

F-1 Visa: https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/students/f1-students/important

Optional Practical Training: https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/students/f1-students/opt

Curriculum Practical Training: https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/students/f1-students/cpt

10.2 Airfare

Airfare could be expensive. While there is one great American student website that could save you a lot of money.

Website: https://www.studentuniverse.com/

10.3 Telecommunication Provider Set-up

AT&T is a generally accepted telecommunication provider in U.S., offering services from Internet, TV network and phone-call data.

AT&T Ann Arbor Location: 3217 Washtenaw Boulevard, Ann Arbor, MI 48104

Website: https://www.att.com/

10.4 Bank Account Set-up

Chase is one of the most renowned banking institutions in U.S. Here is a list of locations you can go in Ann Arbor to set up your U.S. bank account.

Location Information: https://chase.banklocationmaps.com/en/usa/michigan/ann-arbor

Online Banking Platform: https://www.chase.com
